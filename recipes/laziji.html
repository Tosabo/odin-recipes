La Zi Ji (Chinese Chicken)

Description

Sichuan's most famous chicken dish has landed at Odin's Recipes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Potato Starch
  • Spring Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger

Directions

  1. Cut the chicken into bitesize cubes. Mix with all Group 1 ingredients. Marinate for 20 minutes (or longer if you wish). Remove ginger and spring onion before deep frying.

  4. Leave about 1 tablespoon of oil in the wok. Fry ginger, garlic and Sichuan pepper over a medium low heat for 1 minute. Add dried chillies. Retain the heat level and fry until fragrant (do not burn).
